

**1900-1910:**

-   Quantum theory and atomic structure
-   X-ray crystallography
-   Mendelian genetics
-   Early studies in psychology and behaviorism

**1910-1920:**

-   General theory of relativity
-   Psychoanalysis
-   Development of antibiotics
-   Early studies in quantum mechanics

**1920-1930:**

-   Quantum mechanics and wave-particle duality
-   Behaviorism and conditioning
-   Big Bang theory and expanding universe
-   Early studies in penicillin

**1930-1940:**

-   Discovery of nuclear fission
-   Human genetics and inheritance patterns
-   Development of radar technology
-   Formation of Earth's magnetic field

**1940-1950:**

-   World War II-related research (nuclear weapons, radar, cryptography)
-   DNA structure and genetics
-   Development of computer technology
-   Early studies in artificial intelligence

**1950-1960:**

-   Development of the first electronic computers
-   Space exploration and the space race
-   Discovery of the structure of DNA
-   Studies in cybernetics and information theory

**1960-1970:**

-   Civil rights movement and social sciences research
-   Moon landing and space exploration
-   Environmental science and conservation
-   Development of laser technology

**1970-1980:**

-   Advances in molecular biology and genetic engineering
-   Early studies in HIV/AIDS
-   Development of personal computers
-   Climate change and global warming research

**1980-1990:**

-   Human genome project and DNA sequencing
-   Breakthroughs in nanotechnology
-   Internet and World Wide Web development
-   Studies in chaos theory and complexity

**1990-2000:**

-   Biotechnology and genetic engineering advancements
-   Rise of the internet and e-commerce
-   Stem cell research
-   Early studies in artificial life and robotics

**2000-2010:**

-   Genomics and personalized medicine
-   Renewable energy and sustainability
-   Social media and online communities
-   Climate change and mitigation strategies

**2010-2020:**

-   Artificial intelligence and machine learning
-   CRISPR gene editing technology
-   Cybersecurity and data privacy
-   Renewable energy technologies and energy storage

Please note that this list is a broad overview, and there are numerous other significant research topics that have emerged throughout the years. The popularity and focus of research can vary across different fields and disciplines.
